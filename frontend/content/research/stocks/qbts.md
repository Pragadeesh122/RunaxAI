## Thesis
The market trades all four quantum pure-plays (IONQ / RGTI / QBTS / QUBT) as one undifferentiated basket. **D-Wave is the differentiated one within the speculative sleeve**: it is the only pure-play with (a) a *fielded, commercially-deployed* product — quantum **annealing** aimed at real optimization problems (logistics, scheduling, finance); (b) **actual system sales to named customers** (a **$20M** Advantage2 sale to Florida Atlantic University; the industry's first **$10M** enterprise QCaaS license); and (c) a **$588M cash fortress** = multi-year runway and the **lowest near-term dilution risk** of the group. Variant view: own QBTS as the *relative-preference* quantum holding — the "real revenue + best balance sheet" name — rather than chasing the gate-model lottery tickets. This is **not** a high-conviction long: it is an asymmetric, tiny-size speculative bet, not a core position.
