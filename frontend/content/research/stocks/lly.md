## Thesis

Lilly is the structural winner of the GLP-1 / obesity buildout, and today's catalyst widens the moat. The **Medicare GLP-1 Bridge Program** (launches **Jul 1, 2026**) gives eligible Part D patients **Zepbound** plus the new oral **Foundayo (orforglipron)** at **$50/month** — the first broad Medicare coverage of a GLP-1 for *obesity* (Lilly pegs ~20M eligible patients). With orforglipron (oral, retail-pharmacy scalable) the bottleneck shifts from access to manufacturing. The franchise call is consensus, so the edge is **refusing to chase a +6.9% ATH at ~40x trailing** where per-script economics compress ($50 Medicare / ~$350 DTC vs >$1,000 list). Volume TAM is re-rating faster than price-per-patient is falling — bullish for absolute profit *dollars* — but the multiple already prices flawless execution. This is a NEW initiation with an entry plan, not a buy-at-market.
