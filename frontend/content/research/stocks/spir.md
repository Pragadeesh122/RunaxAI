## Thesis (variant view)

The Street still optically prices it as a shrinking business (Q1-26 revenue **−34% YoY**), but that headline is the **maritime-business sale distortion**; the **retained** core data + space-services line is guided to **>50% YoY growth** at the FY26 midpoint with improving margins. With Rocket Lab announcing an **~$8B acquisition of Iridium today (06-29)**, Spire screens as a sub-$700M **consolidation candidate / cheap recurring-data exposure** versus a richly-valued Planet Labs (PL) — a contrarian way to own the space-data theme that just re-rated on M&A. This is an asymmetric, spec-sized variant long, not a fundamentals-anchored buy.
