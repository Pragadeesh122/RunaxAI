## Thesis

The market prices NVO as if it is in terminal decline — de-rated hard on the Dec-2024 CagriSema "miss," two guidance cuts, and a Goldman downgrade, while **LLY trades at ~30x+**. The variant view: this is the **#2 franchise in the fastest-growing category in pharma**, trading at a **single-digit-to-low-teens multiple (TTM P/E 11.1x) of an earnings base that is still growing and beating** — Q1-2026 ADR EPS $1.038 vs $0.87 est (a >19% beat), the third straight large beat. Three near-term de-risking legs the bears under-weight: (1) **oral Wegovy** (oral semaglutide 25mg) received a **CHMP positive opinion in the EU** — the first oral GLP-1 recommended for weight management — opening a needle-free, capacity-light growth leg; (2) **U.S. Medicare GLP-1 coverage begins Jul 1, 2026**, a structural TAM expansion; (3) **recent legal wins** (S. Africa Gauteng High Court, Jun 22 2026) on compounding/IP. You are paid to wait via a cheap multiple and a buyback shrinking the share count underneath. **WATCH, not a higher conviction**, because the H2 U.S. FDA outcomes and LLY's oral threat are genuinely binary — initiate on the watch-list and size only on catalyst confirmation.
