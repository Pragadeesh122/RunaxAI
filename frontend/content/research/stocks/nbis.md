## Valuation basis

- **P/E is meaningless here** (75.66, flattered by investment/legacy-stake gains, not core neocloud ops, which remain pre-profit and heavy-capex). Anchor on revenue/backlog instead.

No clean listed neocloud comp at this scale — valuation is execution/optionality-driven, which is exactly why conviction is capped at WATCH.
